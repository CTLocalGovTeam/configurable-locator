How to use the Water Access Locator

The Water Access Locator application helps citizens locate a marina, fishing site or boat ramp and obtain information about activities within their community. This application is typically used by residents within a State, but can be used by visitors to locate a water access location near them, or one that offers the activity they are interested in.

To locate a marina, fishing site or boat ramp simply enter an address, or marina, fishing site or boat ramp name in the search box or select activities. Or, you can use your current location to find a marina, fishing site or boat ramp near you. The marina, fishing site or boat ramp(s) will then be highlighted on the map and relevant information about available activities presented to you.

Using the Application

The Water Access Locator application provides residents and visitors an easy way to find marina, fishing site or boat ramps near them, a marina, fishing site or boat ramp by its name, or recreation opportunities offered in areas within the State. Enter your address or click on the Current Location button to find marina, fishing site or boat ramps near you. If you click the Current Location button, it will use the location of your computer's IP address or your cell phone/tablet location to locate marina, fishing site or boat ramps near you. When you enter an address or use your current location, the application will present a list of marina, fishing site or boat ramps near you and allow you to review relevant information about each. In addition, you can search for a marina, fishing site or boat ramp, or specific recreation activity you'd like to undertake, by name. To do that, enter the name of the marina, fishing site or boat ramp in the search box. When you enter the name, the application will find the marina, fishing site or boat ramp or present a list of marina, fishing site or boat ramps that offer the recreation activity you're interested in and allow you to review relevant information about each.

Once you have found a water access location you are interested in, click the "Add to My List" button in the info-popup window or in the information panel. Once marinas, fishing sites or boat ramps have been added to your list, you can view that list by clicking the "My List" button. From there, you can calculate a route between them or print your list for later use.

Finding an Area of Interest

There are three primary ways to identify an area of interest. You can interact directly with the map and click on a marina, fishing site or boat ramp when you've located it. You can also use your current location or an address to find one near you. Finally, you can search for a marina, fishing site or boat ramp by name.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Use the basemap switcher to select the basemap you'd like to interact with
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold Down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Click the + or - to zoom in/out

Searching

The search box can be used to find an address or marina, fishing site or boat ramp and recreation area you're interested in:

1. Enter the address or marina, fishing site or boat ramp name you're interested in
2. Click on the item in the list to locate the address or click on the marina, fishing site or boat ramp in the list on the left panel

You can enter a partial address and then identify the correct value in the list.

The Activities panel can be used to search for marina, fishing site or boat ramps by recrational activities:

1. Click on each activity you are intersted in and click search.

Recreation Activities

When you find a marina, fishing site or boat ramp you're interested in, you can select it from the list and it will then be highlighted on the map. Additional detail about each marina, fishing site or boat ramp and the recreational activities available will be provided in the information panel. When you click on an individual marina, fishing site or boat ramp on the map, the same information will be displayed.

Directions

You can also get directions to each marina, fishing site or boat ramp using your current location or an address you select. When you find a marina, fishing site or boat ramp you'd like to visit, enter an address to start from, or click the use current location button, to calculate the directions and route.

Share Your Map

You can click on the Share Map button to share your map several different ways. You can compose an email, post to your Facebook wall or post a Tweet. When you use the Facebook or Twitter links, you'll be asked to sign in to your respective account.

More Information

The Water Access Locator application can used by State Fish and Wildlife, State DNR, State Tourism or other State government organizations responsible for managing marina, fishing site or boat ramps to deploy a web-based marina, fishing site or boat ramp locator application. This application provides 24x7 access to the organization and typically supplements customer service phone numbers staffed by these organizations.
